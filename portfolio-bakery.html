About the piece

Taximaroa Bakery is a traditional Mexican Panaderia serving the Northwest side of Chicago for over 30 years. In 2020, they were looking for a fresh look to welcome the next generation of warm breads & sweet treats lovers. What's a panaderia without a concha? In this logo I used the mouthwatering sweet bread as the star of the show.
