About the pieces

Focusing on the beauty of the calligraphy, I had the pleasure of creating these watercolor inspired paper goods for the beautiful garden wedding of Jasper and Michael Walker. Printed on soft linen, this elegant wedding suite focused on the simpisitic of cool colors and clean negative space. This gorgeous wedding was featured on Style Me Pretty wedding blog!
