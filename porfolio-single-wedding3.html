About the pieces

A unique wedding for a creative couple. Professional photographer, Jill, and author of The Walking Dead, Jay, couldn't have anything less than a picture perfect, story book romance. The invitation suite even had a story! A glossary of yiddish terms, love quotes, and a drawing done by the mother of the bride serving as the focal point, made this wedding suite one of my favorite wedding projects to date. Jill and Jay's romantic day was featured in Offbeat Bride!
