About the pieces

As a designer, the most exciting part about planning my own wedding was, of course, the invitation suite! I pulled together watercolor elements to create a sense of softness and elegance. The RSVP card featured unique icons tailored to represent both mine and my husband's personalities. Additionally, a hand-drawn map highlighted fun facts about us and tips on places to visit for our out-of-town guest.
